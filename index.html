A Legacy of Performance

BMW, founded in 1916, is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Renowned for its engineering, driving dynamics, and design, BMW is a leading premium automobile brand.

BMW's Rich History

BMW began with aircraft engines in 1916, officially becoming Bavarian Motor Works in 1917. Automobile production started in 1928.

Iconic BMW Models

BMW has created numerous legendary vehicles over the decades.

The BMW 3 Series

Introduced in 1975, the 3 Series is BMW's best-selling model, known for its sporty handling and driver-focused design.

The BMW 7 Series

The 7 Series is BMW's flagship luxury sedan, showcasing advanced technology and comfort.

The BMW M Division

BMW M GmbH produces high-performance versions of BMW cars, engineered for enthusiasts.

  • Iconic M Models: M3, M5, M2, M4

Innovation and the Future

BMW focuses on electrification, autonomous driving, and sustainable manufacturing. The "i" sub-brand leads its electric mobility efforts.

The BMW i Series

Models like the iX and i4 represent BMW's fully electric future, combining zero-emission driving with signature performance and luxury.
